# Quick Start - Get Your Live Link in 2 Minutes

Follow these simple steps to get your portfolio live and get a shareable link.

## Option 1: Netlify (Recommended)

**Step 1:** Click this link → [Deploy to Netlify](https://app.netlify.com/start/deploy?repository=https://github.com/pulkitrais/auto-annotated-portfolio)

**Step 2:** Sign in with your GitHub account (it's free)

**Step 3:** Click "Save & Deploy"

**Step 4:** Wait 2-3 minutes and get your link: `https://[your-site-name].netlify.app`

**That's it!** Share your link with anyone.

## Option 2: Vercel

**Step 1:** Go to [vercel.com/new](https://vercel.com/new)

**Step 2:** Import repository: `pulkitrais/auto-annotated-portfolio`

**Step 3:** Click "Deploy"

**Step 4:** Get your link: `https://[project-name].vercel.app`

## What You Get

After deployment:
- ✅ Live portfolio website
- ✅ Shareable link (HTTPS enabled)
- ✅ Auto-updates when you push code
- ✅ Free hosting
- ✅ Custom domain support (optional)
